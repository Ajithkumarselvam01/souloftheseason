RERA-Approved Investment Transparency Security

Soul of the Seasons is applied for RERA approval, ensuring legal compliance, quality assurance, and buyer protection. Investing in a RERA-approved property offers several advantages:

  • Security: 70% of the buyers funds are secured in an escrow account, ensuring financial transparency.
  • Accountability: Developers must adhere to approved plans and deliver within committed timelines.
  • Quality Assurance: Structural defects are covered by the builder for five years.
  • Fair Pricing: Properties are sold based on the actual carpet area, eliminating misleading super-built-up charges.
